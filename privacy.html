01 / Introduction

Welcome to IanFit (collectively referred to as "we," "us," "our," or "IanFit"). We provide ultra-accessible, educational physical fitness training, athletic rehabilitation, biomechanics instruction, metabolic advice, and related software systems via our portal.

This Privacy Policy details how we handle the accumulation of personal identifiers, health markers, biomechanical performance video recordings, dietary journals, and tracking diagnostics when you utilize IanFit systems. Accessing our training structures indicates your operational understanding and consent to these terms.

02 / Information We Collect

We separate collected data into two operational classifications to uphold user safety:

A. Voluntary Biometric & Physical Metrics

To establish corrective exercise protocols, we collect detailed indicators of your kinetic patterns:

  • Form & Motion Records: Video captures of gait, running cycles, structural hinge motions, and weightlifting profiles.
  • Metabolic Demands & Core Stats: Age, biological mass, target physical metrics, self-reported energy thresholds, and functional limitations.
  • Injury History & Medical Summaries: Reports on acute/chronic bone, joint, or myofascial issues shared during intake assessments.
  • Dietary Diagnostics: Historical food records, caloric patterns, nutrient preferences, and gut-health surveys.

B. Account Identifiers & Payment Data

Standard transactional variables required to manage customer relationships:

  • Basic Identifiers: Legal name, mailing street, active email, telephone contact, and emergency details.
  • Payment Integrity: All card processing is securely isolated through PCI-compliant providers (e.g., Stripe). IanFit servers do not store raw card numbers.

03 / Usage of Biological Metrics

We process your metrics to design highly personalized lifestyle adaptations. Your biological profile is processed specifically to:

  • Perform biomechanics assessments to identify alignment faults and movement friction.
  • Design customized routines targeting permanent pain alleviation and muscular balance.
  • Estimate real-time metabolic expenditures, aerobic thresholds, and performance targets.
  • Provide personalized feedback on joint mechanics and postural control during core movements (walking, lifting, running).
  • Refine custom calorie and nutrient recommendations based on your metabolic demands.

04 / Sharing Protocol

We do not share your movement, nutrition, or physical profile with external third parties for advertising or product positioning. Sharing is limited to the following exceptions:

  • Authorized Integrations: If you intentionally opt in to share metrics with connected fitness platforms (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin Connect), we transfer authorized data packets strictly in accordance with your settings.
  • Legal Safety & Compelled Disclosures: We may share personal data if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of other users.
  • Internal Personnel: Select coaches and trainers under contract with IanFit access client folders solely to review movement mechanics and optimize training routines.

05 / Hardened Security & Safeguards

Our data security architecture is built to safeguard your physiological and personal information. Security protocols include:

  • Mandatory HTTPS/TLS encryption across all forms, video portals, and administrative systems.
  • AES 256-bit encryption for stored data (including biomechanics logs and custom routines).
  • Regular reviews of our database access permissions to protect against unauthorized entry.

06 / Your Rights & Options

You retain full control over your biological profile and physical metrics. Your rights include:

Data Export

Receive a structured export of all stored metrics, movement analyses, and assessment notes.

Account Deletion

Permanently remove your biological profile, weight records, and videos from our active servers within 30 days of request.

To submit a data request, please email privacy@ianfit.com with the subject line "Data Sovereignty Request."

07 / Tracking & Cookies

We use cookies and tracking tools to improve system performance and assess user engagement. Cookies help us analyze training module progress, track page views, and save your theme selection (Sun/Moon).

You can disable cookies through your web browser's settings. However, disabling cookies may impact certain interactive features on our site.

08 / Contact Information

For questions regarding our privacy practices, health data protection, or kinetic video storage, please contact our support team.
